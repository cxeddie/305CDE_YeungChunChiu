Stimulants

Amphetamines: Methamphetamine, also known as methylamphetamine:

Substance: Methamphetamine tablet

Street Names: Yaba

Substance: Crystal methamphetamine

Street Names: Ice

Substance: Phentermine(Duromine, Redusa, Mirapront)

Street Names: None

Medical Use: None

Effect and Danger: 11. Insomnia 2. Agitation 3. Panic and delirium 4. Anxiety and tension 5. Skin sores 6. Depression 7. Heart failure 8. Hallucination and feeling of persecution which may lead to violent behaviour 9. In very high doses, toxic psychosis, convulsions, coma, brain haemorrhage and death may occur

Substance: Cocaine

Street Names: C, coke, flake, snow, stardust, Charlie, crack, gold dust, coco

Medical Use: Local or topical anaesthetic

Effect and Danger: 1. Addiction 2. Respiratory damage 3. Mania 4. Paranoid delusion 5. Impaired memory 6. Hallucination 7. Delirium 8. Heart disease 9. Nasal damage

Substance: Methylenedioxy-methamphetamine (MDMA)

Street Names: Ecstasy

Medical Use: None

Effect and Danger: 1. Tending to over-exercise which may lead to exhaustion, over-heating and eventually convulsion or collapse 2. Causing convulsions of muscles and uncontrollable behaviour of "shaking and dancing" 3. Confusion, depression, sleep problem, anxiety and paranoia, such feelings may last for weeks after taking the drug 4. Increase in heart rate and blood pressure poses a special risk for people with circulatory or heart disease 5. Hot crowded conditions, e.g. party sites, can lead to dehydration and heart or kidney failure 6. Abusers may undermine safe sex practices due to reduction in inhibitions

Substance: Derivatives of cathinone, e.g Mephedrone

Street Names: Meow Meow

Medical Use: None

Effect and Danger: 1. Altered level of consciousness 2. Memory problems and blood circulation problems 3. Erratic behaviour, hallucinations and delusions
