About us

Eddie’s Anti-drug Information and Resources are established in 2016 with the following aims:

  1. To promote public education against drug abuse.
  2. To consult, advise, or co-operate with any other person or organisation in furthering the aims of the Society.
  3. undertaking any research into the problems of drug abuse.
  4. encouraging, participating in, and supporting community education of the problems and preventing of drug abuse.
