Hallucinogens

Hallucinogens

Substance: Cannabis

Street Names: Marijuana, hash, grass

Medical Use: None

Effect and Danger: 1. Hallucination 2. Impaired co-ordination 3. Addiction 4. Heightened risk of cancer 5. Reduced concentration and impaired memory and judgment 6. Depression and extreme suspicion of others 7. Irritability and short temper 8. Respiratory disease 9. Regular users of cannabis are more likely to try other illicit drugs

Substance: Cannabis resin

Street Names: None

Medical Use: None

Effect and Danger: 1. Hallucination 2. Impaired co-ordination 3. Addiction 4. Heightened risk of cancer 5. Reduced concentration and impaired memory and judgment 6. Depression and extreme suspicion of others 7. Irritability and short temper 8. Respiratory disease 9. Regular users of cannabis are more likely to try other illicit drugs

Substance: Synthetic cannabinoids

Street Names: K2, SPICE

Medical Use: None

Effect and Danger: 1. Hallucinations 2. Increased agitation 3. Elevated blood pressure and heart rates 4. Seizures

Substance: LSD

Street Names: Black Sesame, Fing Ba

Medical Use: None

Effect and Danger: 1. Disoriented behaviour 2. Impaired judgement 3. Nausea and vomiting 4. Increased pulse rate and elevated blood pressure
